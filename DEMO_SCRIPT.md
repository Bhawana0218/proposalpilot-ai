# Demo Video Script (target: 4 minutes)

Use this as talking points, not a script to read verbatim — sound natural.

**0:00–0:20 — Positioning (don't open the app yet)**
"This is ProposalPilot AI — not a proposal generator, a presales copilot.
Most solutions to this brief will be a form, one AI call, and a PDF. I built
the parts of presales that are usually invisible: discovery questions,
scope-creep detection, and a completeness/deal-quality score an account
manager would actually use before hitting send."

**0:20–1:00 — Client intake (screen: StepClient → StepGoals → StepServices)**
Fill in a real-feeling example fast (e.g. a mid-size e-commerce client
wanting a chatbot + mobile app + analytics — enough to trigger scope-creep
later). Narrate: "Company info, business goals in their own words, and
requested services — select 4 to make the scope-creep detector interesting."

**1:00–1:40 — AI Discovery Interview Agent**
"Before it writes anything, Claude reviews this intake like a consultant on
a discovery call and asks what's actually missing." Answer 1-2 questions,
skip the rest. "This is the difference between generating a proposal and
consulting on one."

**1:40–2:40 — Generated proposal walkthrough**
Point at, in order: completeness meter, scope-creep alert (explain the
phasing suggestion), the internal-only quality score + deal probability
panel ("this never reaches the client — it's the sales team's signal"),
then scroll the dossier itself: executive summary → scope → risks →
timeline → team → architecture → pricing tiers with the recommended one
highlighted → ROI.

**2:40–3:10 — Proposal Chat Assistant**
Ask it live: "Why is development estimated at [X] weeks?" and let it answer
from the proposal's own numbers. "This makes the document defensible in a
client call, not just readable."

**3:10–3:40 — PDF export**
Click export, open the PDF. Point out: cover page, table of contents,
signature block. "Built with jsPDF directly against the same data — not a
screenshot of the page, so it's real selectable text and paginates
properly."

**3:40–4:00 — Close**
"Architecture and prompt design are in ARCHITECTURE.md and README.md — the
three API routes and prompts.ts are the core of the AI implementation.
Thanks for reviewing."
